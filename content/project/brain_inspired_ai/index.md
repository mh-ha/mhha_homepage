**Research Scientist** | CNAI, KAIST | Nov. 2019--Dec. 2023

- Led the design of a simulation model for cognitive brain development.
- Discovered that noise-tuning alignment of latent variables is a reliable predictor of generalization performance (CNS 2023).
- Investigated regional Lipschitz continuity and its role in the generalization-robustness trade-off.
- Proposed an invariant representation learning framework for stochastic neural networks inspired by metamerism in the human visual cortex.
